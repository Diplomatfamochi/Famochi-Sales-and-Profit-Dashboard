## ğŸ› Business Context & Challenge (Situation)

Famochi is a multi-category retail organization operating across **Office Supplies, Furniture, Technology, and Consumables**.  
Despite having extensive transactional data from **January 2021 onward**, leadership lacked a **single source of truth** for performance monitoring.

### Key Challenges
- Sales and profit data were locked in raw transactional formats  
- Difficulty distinguishing **high-revenue vs high-profit** segments  
- Limited visibility into **customer growth and retention trends**  
- Manual reporting limited strategic and tactical planning

### Primary Stakeholders

- Sales Leadership  
- Finance & Operations Teams
